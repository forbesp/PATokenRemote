Grevilleas are the most beautiful native plants of all and attract all honey eating birds.

Proteas are lovely plants that you will enjoy growing.

Banksias attract many birds and they look great in the garden

Acacias are beautiful and the Golden Wattle is the National Floral Emblem.

Grasses make lovely borders.
